1000s calories Smoothie

Description:

This is a 1000s calories easy to make smoothie.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups of Milk 2%
  • 1 Cup of Oats
  • 2 Scoop of protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon of peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1 banana
  • Optional: More frozen fruits!

Steps:

Note: Since you are going to blend it, it does not really matter the order you put it.

  1. Put all the ingredients in blender
  2. Blend!
